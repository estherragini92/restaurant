HOME RECIPES BLOG ABOUT ME

I’m Tiffy (Tiffany) a born and “bread” 20-something South African gal. I’m a food lover, self-taught chef, baker, food photographer & videographer. I started my blog as a hobby in lockdown 1.0, but it has since become my full-time job and biggest passion!

My main inspiration for sharing easy and healthy recipes comes from my past of disordered, restrictive eating habits and my struggles with a hormonal disorder – PCOS.

I’m at my happiest when helping & serving others and I hope that my recipes can help YOU make healthier choices, without the “FAD” diets and restriction, but instead by making recipes using healthier ingredients.

Quick Fire Facts:

  • ➡️ I became a Christian 3 years ago and it’s completely turned my life around.
  • ➡️ I grew up in Cape Town, but moved to England at 18.
  • ➡️ I went on my first solo trip to Europe for 6 weeks.
  • ➡️ I’m happiest when helping & serving others.
  • ➡️ My death-row meal: sourdough with butter & cheddar.
  • ➡️ I have PCOS and focus on healthy eating.
  • ➡️ I struggled with disordered eating but I’m improving.

✨ “My mission is to motivate you to nourish your body without depriving yourself, but instead by making mindful choices and finding balance.” ✨
